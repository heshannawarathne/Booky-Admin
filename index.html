Dispatch Guidelines
Action Irreversible: Once a notification is dispatched, it cannot be recalled or edited.
  • Broadcast: Use for general updates like new route openings, holiday schedules, or app-wide announcements.
  • Targeted: Strictly for trip-specific alerts such as delays, vehicle breakdowns, or departure platform changes.
  • Clarity: Keep the "Message Body" short and direct. Always include a reason if a trip is delayed or cancelled.
  • Accuracy: Double-check the selected Date and Bus Number before clicking dispatch to avoid notifying the wrong passengers.
